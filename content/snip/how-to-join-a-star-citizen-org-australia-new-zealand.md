# How to Join a Star Citizen Org in Australia & New Zealand

Star Citizen is huge, and flying solo can mean odd hours and quiet skies.

If you’re playing in Australia or New Zealand, joining a community that’s active when you are makes everything easier—from organised events to casual crew nights.

---

## Why join an ANZ Star Citizen org?

Timezone overlap matters.

When AU/NZ evenings line up, you get:
- fuller parties  
- clearer comms  
- fewer “everyone’s offline” moments  

Events happen at sensible local times, so group play feels natural instead of a scheduling puzzle.

## What makes SCANZ different?

SCANZ is **community-first**.

We’re casual, welcoming, and built for real life. There’s no ego, no strict roles, and no pressure to play a certain way. Whether you’re exploring, running missions, or just figuring things out, you’ll find people to fly with.

## How to join a Star Citizen org in Australia & New Zealand

Joining SCANZ is simple:

1. **Join Discord** — This is where everything happens: comms, events, and general chaos.
2. **Apply via RSI** — Use the official org page when you’re ready.
3. **Include your Discord name** if it’s different from your in-game name so we can match things up quickly.

## What happens after you join?

Once you’re in, you’ll:
- get access to the full Discord  
- meet the crew and jump into active channels  
- join events, ops, or casual gameplay  
- receive onboarding guidance if you’re new  

You can participate straight away—no waiting around.

Yes.
