# SCANZ Screenshot Competition — Capture the Chaos

Some moments in Star Citizen are too good to be explained.

So… screenshot them.

---

## How it works

This runs over **4 weeks**, with submissions happening continuously.

Each week:
- Post your screenshots in our Discord **#media channel**  
  👉 **[Join the SCANZ Discord](/join)**  
- The **Custodians and Officers** will pick **1–2 winners**

By the end, we’ll have **8 finalists**.

Then we hand it over to the community for a final vote to decide the overall winner.

---

## What we’re looking for

Honestly? No idea.

There’s only one real rule:
❌ **No AI-generated images** — keep it in-game

Everything else is fair game:

- Cinematic shots  
- Dumb moments  
- Absolute chaos  
- Perfect timing  
- Things that probably shouldn’t have worked  

If it feels like SCANZ, it’s in the running.

Submit as many as you like.

---

## The prize

The winning screenshot gets turned into a **custom SCANZ mouse pad**.

- 🥇 Winner gets one **for free**
- 🛒 Community members can grab one too if they want

So yes — your dumbest moment could live forever on someone’s desk.

---

## Why this exists

Because SCANZ isn’t about perfect gameplay.

It’s about the moments in between:
- The near misses  
- The accidental wins  
- The completely avoidable disasters  

This is your chance to capture that.
