# Star Citizen 4.8 Tactical Strike Groups: What to Expect & How SCANZ Is Preparing

**Tactical Strike Groups** are coming in *Star Citizen 4.8*, bringing a new level of structured, multi-phase fleet gameplay.

Currently live in **Wave 1 PTU**, this system is already proving to be less about raw firepower — and more about coordination, logistics, and execution.

SCANZ members are already in the PTU, testing the activity, gathering intel, and preparing to run full-scale operations when it releases more broadly.

---

## What Are Tactical Strike Groups?

Tactical Strike Groups are **multi-phase cooperative missions** designed for organised fleets.

Success depends on more than just showing up with guns:

- **Fleet composition** matters (fighters, support, logistics, medical)
- **Loadouts** need to match the objective
- **Sustain** is critical (fuel, ammo, medical support)
- **Comms** need to be clear and intentional

This is a full operation — not a single fight.

---

## Tactical Strike Group Mission Phases

Based on current 4.8 PTU testing, missions follow a structured series of objectives:

### Phase 1
- **Space Combat** — *Defeat Attacking Fighters*

### Phase 2
- **Space & FPS Combat** — *Assault Station*

### Phase 3
- **Space Combat** — *Intercept Bombers*

### Phase 4
- **Space & FPS Combat** — *Destroy Power Core*

### Phase 5
- **Space Combat** — *Save Pilot*

### Phase 6
- **Space Combat** — *Save Pilot*  
  *(If the pilot dies, the mission fails)*

Each phase shifts the focus between fleet combat, ground operations, and objective control — forcing teams to adapt quickly.

---

## SCANZ PTU Testing & Intel Gathering

SCANZ already has members actively running Tactical Strike Groups in **Wave 1 PTU**.

Right now, the focus is on:

- Building **reliable fleet compositions** for each phase  
- Testing **refuelling, rearming, and medical workflows**  
- Refining **comms structure** for multi-role coordination  
- Identifying **failure points** (especially pilot survival phases)  

This early testing means we’re not going in blind when 4.8 hits wider release.

---

## How SCANZ Will Run Tactical Strike Groups

When Tactical Strike Groups roll out to more players, SCANZ will be running **organised fleet operations**, built around:

- **Defined roles** — pilots, gunners, logistics, FPS teams  
- **Planned fleet comps** — not “bring whatever”  
- **Structured comms** — clear calls, minimal noise  
- **On-site sustain** — keeping ships and players operational  

The goal isn’t just to attempt the mission — it’s to **complete it consistently**.
